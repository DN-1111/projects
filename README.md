# 🚀 Projects Overview


### 🌱 Enterprise Energy & Carbon Optimization Analysis

**Tools:** Python (Pandas), MySQL, Tableau  

- Cleaned and analyzed **7,200+ hourly energy consumption records across 20 buildings**, engineering time-based features and estimating carbon emissions to evaluate sustainability impact.  
- Performed **SQL-based operational analysis** to benchmark energy usage by building, building type, and occupancy level, identifying evening peak demand patterns (**20:00–22:00**) and potential base-load inefficiencies during low occupancy periods.  
- Developed a **three-page interactive Tableau dashboard** visualizing energy consumption trends, operational load behavior, and sustainability metrics.  
- Identified opportunities for **renewable energy adoption**, demonstrating a potential **~20% carbon reduction (~190 tCO₂)** and supporting data-driven sustainability planning.
___

### 📊 Service Operations SLA Performance Analysis

**Tools:** MySQL, Tableau, Excel  

- Analyzed **51,000+ customer complaint records**, creating SLA eligibility and breach indicators to evaluate service response performance across financial service operations.  
- Performed **SQL-based operational analysis** to identify SLA breach patterns across products, issue categories, and U.S. states, highlighting key operational bottlenecks.  
- Built an **interactive Tableau dashboard** featuring KPI monitoring, product risk analysis, issue root-cause insights, geographic complaint distribution, and complaint trends to support service improvement decisions.

___

### 🧸 Inventory Imbalance & Demand Analysis

**Tools:** Python (Pandas, NumPy), Tableau  

- Analyzed **7,000+ retail transactions across 35 products and 50 stores**, merging sales, product, and store datasets and engineering revenue and time-based demand features.  
- Performed product and store performance analysis to identify **demand concentration and location-specific demand patterns**, highlighting potential stock-out and excess inventory risks.  
- Built aggregated analytical datasets and developed an **interactive Tableau dashboard** visualizing KPIs, top products, store performance, demand heat maps, and monthly sales trends to support data-driven inventory planning.

___


### 🕵️ Patterns of Deception: Credit Card Fraud Analysis

**Tools:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn), Tableau, PowerPoint  

- Cleaned and analyzed **284K+ credit card transactions**, discovering that **50%+ of frauds occur in low-value (<€10) transactions and off-peak hours**.  
- Built a **KPI-driven Tableau dashboard** to visualize fraud trends, transaction ranges, and PCA feature correlations (V1–V28).  
- Delivered strategic recommendations including **enhanced off-peak monitoring and tighter controls for small-value transactions**.

### 🚗 Road Crash Severity & Pattern Analysis
